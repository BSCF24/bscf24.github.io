Future STEM Leaders Programme Launch

The BSCF Future STEM Leaders Programme 2025 Cohort 1 officially began with a successful launch at Rajshahi Novotheatre, marking the start of an exciting journey to nurture and empower the next generation of STEM leaders. The event featured speeches from distinguished academics and researchers of the country, sharing insights, inspiration, and guidance for young participants. The Chief Guest was Professor Dr Robiul Islam Sarker, Director (Student Welfare) of Rajshahi University of Engineering & Technology, while Special Guests included Professor Dr Sajjad Bokul, Professor Dr Ekram Hossain, Dr Habibur Rahman, Oishi Jyoti, and senior consultants Omar Farhan Khan and Nazmul Kabir Al-Mehedi from LightCastle Partners. The ceremony was supported by She-STEM, LightCastle Partners Limited, and Novotheatre, whose collaboration ensured the event’s success. The launch celebrated innovation, inclusivity, and leadership, motivating participants to actively engage, explore STEM fields, and develop skills that will shape a brighter and more empowered future.
