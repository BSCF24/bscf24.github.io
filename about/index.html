Our Story

About BSCF

Bangladesh Socio-Cultural Forum (BSCF) is an independent, non-political, and non-profit platform dedicated to the socio-cultural and educational development of Bangladeshi youth.

We focus on enhancing social skills, encouraging participation in cultural activities, promoting environmental awareness, and fostering tolerance and personal growth.

Our Vision

BSCF envisions a Bangladesh where youth empowerment is paramount, ensuring their active participation and influential voice in national development. We advocate for a tolerant nation, emphasizing empathy and respect for diverse religions, perspectives, and beliefs.

Our goal is to cultivate socially responsible, environmentally conscious citizens with cultural proficiency and a global mindset.

Our Mission

BSCF engages youth from diverse backgrounds—villages, urban areas, grassroots, and underprivileged regions—offering tangible behavioral skills, social responsibility, mental health awareness, and cultural exchange opportunities.

We empower youth through volunteerism, fostering an environment where they emerge as non-communal, respectful, humble, and tolerant global citizens advocating for sustainable development.

BSCF is a socio-cultural forum for the youth of Bangladesh, dedicated to fostering a generation of leaders and innovators. We aim to create a vibrant community that promotes our rich cultural heritage while embracing modernity and progress.
