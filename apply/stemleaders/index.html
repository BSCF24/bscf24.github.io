STEM LEADERS 2025

BSCF Future STEM Leaders Programme 2025 – Cohort 1 In collaboration with Rajshahi Novotheatre and SheSTEM, Bangladesh Socio-Cultural Forum (BSCF) is launching an exciting 1-month internship programme for STEM students from 3rd year undergraduate to postgraduate and even recent Master’s graduates. 🎯 90% of the participants will be female students, creating a platform to inspire and empower future women leaders in STEM. 🚀 The programme will include: Professional journey & leadership sessions Research methodology & HR management Hands-on telescope training & innovation workshops Certificate as Technical Trainee upon successful completion 📌 This is an unpaid opportunity, but a life-changing experience to strengthen your skills, networks, and future career. 🗓 Application Deadline: 11:59PM, 13 September 2025
