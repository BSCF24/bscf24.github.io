About BSCF

Bangladesh Socio-Cultural Forum (BSCF) is an independent, non-political, and non-profit platform dedicated to the socio-cultural and educational development of Bangladeshi youth. We focus on enhancing social skills, encouraging participation in cultural activities, promoting environmental awareness, and fostering tolerance and personal growth.

Our Vision

BSCF envisions a Bangladesh where youth empowerment is paramount, ensuring their active participation and influential voice in national development. We advocate for a tolerant nation, emphasizing empathy and respect for diverse religions, perspectives, and beliefs. Our goal is to cultivate socially responsible, environmentally conscious citizens with cultural proficiency and a global mindset, ready to engage with the world and contribute to volunteerism, service, and long-term employment opportunities.

Our Mission

BSCF engages youth from diverse backgrounds—villages, urban areas, grassroots, and underprivileged regions—offering tangible behavioral skills, social responsibility, mental health awareness, and cultural exchange opportunities. We empower youth through volunteerism, fostering an environment where they emerge as non-communal, respectful, humble, environmentally aware, and tolerant global citizens, advocating for good governance and sustainable development.

Initiatives

BSCF drives various initiatives to empower youth and promote social responsibility.

Nature Center

The Nature Center initiative focuses on environmental awareness and conservation efforts. It provides a platform for youth to engage in activities that promote sustainability and protect Bangladesh's natural heritage.
