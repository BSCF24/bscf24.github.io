About Us

What is BSCF?

BSCF is an independent, non-political platform fostering personal growth and environmental awareness among youth.

We envision a Bangladesh where youth empowerment ensures an influential voice in national development, advocating for a tolerant and socially responsible nation.

Read our full vision →

BSCF is a socio-cultural forum for the youth of Bangladesh, dedicated to fostering a generation of leaders and innovators. We aim to create a vibrant community that promotes our rich cultural heritage while embracing modernity and progress.
