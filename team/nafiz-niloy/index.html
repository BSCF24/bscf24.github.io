Nafis Iqbal Niloy is a creative media enthusiast with a strong interest in photography, cinematography, and visual storytelling. Currently pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Accounting, he combines academic learning with practical experience in creative media production and event coordination. He serves as the Creative Director at the Bangladesh Socio-Cultural Forum (BSCF), where he leads and supervises creative media projects, including promotional videos, branding materials, and visual campaigns. Through this role, he contributes to developing engaging content and managing creative teams for various organizational initiatives. In addition, he is the Founder and Creative Lead of Clicks of Life, a visual media platform specializing in photography and cinematography. Through this initiative, he has gained hands-on experience in directing, media production, and end-to-end photo and video editing projects. His technical expertise includes tools such as Lightroom and CapCut, along with strong skills in professional photography, cinematography, and creative direction.Alongside his technical abilities, Niloy demonstrates strong communication, leadership, and event management skills. His contributions have also been recognized through awards such as the “Best Mentor in Poster Presentation” at BSCF in November 2025. Overall, he continues to develop as a creative professional focused on impactful visual communication and media production.

BSCF is a socio-cultural forum for the youth of Bangladesh, dedicated to fostering a generation of leaders and innovators. We aim to create a vibrant community that promotes our rich cultural heritage while embracing modernity and progress.
