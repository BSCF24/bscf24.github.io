Ahnaf Tahsin Monmoy is an undergraduate student at the University of Rajshahi pursuing a Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA) in Accounting and Information Systems. He is known for his analytical mindset and strong interest in integrating technology with financial and accounting practices to support data-driven decision-making and efficient financial management.Alongside his academic studies, he has demonstrated entrepreneurial initiative as the Founder and CEO of Brogotflix, a streaming service platform, and as the Co-Founder of Mobile Guru, a business operating at Jamuna Future Park. Through these roles, he has gained practical experience in business management, digital media, and financial operations. Ahnaf is also actively engaged in extracurricular and professional communities. He serves as an Associate Member of the Bangladesh Socio-Cultural Forum (BSCF) and the Rajshahi University Professional Development Club, where he participates in leadership and professional development activities. His technical and professional skills include financial management, graphic design, digital media management, and proficiency in Microsoft Office tools.Overall, he aspires to build a career at the intersection of finance, technology, and business innovation, leveraging analytical thinking and technological tools to contribute to the evolving field of accounting and financial systems.

BSCF is a socio-cultural forum for the youth of Bangladesh, dedicated to fostering a generation of leaders and innovators. We aim to create a vibrant community that promotes our rich cultural heritage while embracing modernity and progress.
